If you're experiencing a white page or loading issues, this tool will help clear your browser cache and service worker data.

What this does:

  • Unregisters the service worker
  • Clears all cached data
  • Forces a fresh reload of the site

Manual Steps (if the button doesn't work):

  1. Open Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to Application tab (Chrome) or Storage tab (Firefox)
  3. Find "Service Workers" section
  4. Click "Unregister" for any existing service workers
  5. Go to "Storage" section
  6. Click "Clear site data" or "Clear storage"
  7. Reload the page
